Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What is the server IP?
    A: Java: play.sdpsmp.net, Bedrock: play.sdpsmp.net:19132
  • Q: How do I link my account to the store?
    A: Enter your Minecraft username in the store page, generate a code, then use /link <code> in-game.
  • Q: How can I buy ranks?
    A: After linking, you can use your in-game balance to purchase ranks in the store.
  • Q: What versions of Minecraft are supported?
    A: Java Edition 1.19+ and Bedrock Edition.
  • Q: Who do I contact for support?
    A: Contact a staff member in-game or on Discord.
